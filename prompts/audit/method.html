Comprehensive Scoring Formula

FINAL BRAND SCORE = (ONSITE SCORE × 0.7) + (OFFSITE SCORE × 0.3) × CRISIS MULTIPLIER ONSITE CALCULATION: ├── Tier 1: (Brand Score × 0.8) + (Performance Score × 0.2) ├── Tier 2: (Brand Score × 0.5) + (Performance Score × 0.5) └── Tier 3: (Brand Score × 0.3) + (Performance Score × 0.7) OFFSITE CALCULATION: ├── Owned: (Brand Score × 0.6) + (Performance Score × 0.4) ├── Influenced: (Brand Score × 0.4) + (Authenticity Score × 0.6) └── Independent: (Brand Score × 0.2) + (Sentiment Score × 0.8) CRISIS MULTIPLIER: ├── No Issues: 1.0 (no reduction) ├── Minor Issues: 0.9 (10% reduction) ├── Major Issues: 0.5 (50% reduction) └── Crisis Situation: 0.3 (70% reduction)

Implementation Methodology

1

Comprehensive Site Audit

  • Classify all pages by tier and purpose
  • Evaluate against appropriate rubric
  • Apply progressive weighting system
  • Document brand consistency gaps
2

External Presence Mapping

  • Inventory all external brand touchpoints
  • Classify by control level (Owned/Influenced/Independent)
  • Assess brand alignment and sentiment
  • Monitor reputation indicators
3

Brand Message Integration

  • Verify "The world is how we shape it" corporate positioning
  • Check regional narrative usage ("Secure Progress" for BENELUX)
  • Assess visual brand consistency and human-centric approach
  • Evaluate persona-appropriate sub-narratives
4

Reputation Analysis

  • Monitor Glassdoor ratings and themes
  • Track review sites (G2, Capterra, Clutch)
  • Analyze media coverage and analyst reports
  • Assess competitive positioning
5

Gap Analysis & Prioritization

  • Weight findings by C-suite decision impact
  • Prioritize owned channel optimizations
  • Develop influenced channel strategy
  • Create monitoring for independent channels
6

Continuous Monitoring

  • Establish baseline brand metrics
  • Set up reputation monitoring alerts
  • Track brand consistency improvements
  • Monitor competitive brand positioning
